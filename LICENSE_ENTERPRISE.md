## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What happens if we exceed our user count?**
A: You have a 30-day grace period to either remove users or upgrade your license. After that, overage fees apply.

**Q: Can we use the Enterprise Edition without a license?**
A: No, Enterprise features require a valid license key and active subscription.

**Q: What if we drop below $5M revenue?**
A: You may cancel your Enterprise License and revert to the Community Edition at the end of your term.

**Q: Do you offer educational or non-profit discounts?**
A: Yes! Contact sales@grengin.com with documentation of your status.

**Q: Is the source code available?**
A: The Community Edition is open source. Enterprise Edition source code access is available under a separate agreement.

**Q: What about on-premise/air-gapped deployments?**
A: Fully supported for Enterprise and Enterprise Plus tiers. Special licensing arrangements available for air-gapped environments.
